League Rules

Team Configuration

Each school sends a team of 10 members.

The number of seniors is not to exceed 4.

The sum total of juniors and seniors is not to exceed 8.

Category Partcipation

Each team member participates in 3 of the 6 categories. If a student participates in more than three categories, the three lowest scores are used in determining the individual score.

5 team members compete in each category. If more than five compete, the five lowest scores are used for the team score in that category.

Contest Procedure

NO CALCULATORS ARE ALLOWED IN THE TESTING AREA!

Rounds 1 through 6 are 10 minutes with a 2 minute and a 15 second warning. All writing must stop at this time. The Team Round is 12 minutes with a 2 minute and a 30 second warning.

Before the start of each category, each competitor must write his/her complete name, grade and school on the back of the contest paper. No other writing is allowed until the starting time for the category is announced.

Answers must be legible and placed on designated lines for credit.

When necessary units are required, to receive credit, a student must use these units.

Scoring

Each question in Categories 1 - 6 is worth 2 points. Each question in the Team Round is worth 3 points.

Each student's yearly score is the best five out of six scores in league competitions.

The team's score is the sum of the six competitions.
